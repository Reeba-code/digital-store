Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tlapeng Digital Tuckshop?

Tlapeng Digital Tuckshop is an online store offering a wide range of products including food, beverages, household items, and more. We strive to provide our customers with the best deals and a convenient shopping experience.

How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products, add the items you wish to purchase to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will be guided through the process to complete your order.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including SASSA cards, EFT, and bank transfers. You can select your preferred payment method during the checkout process.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been packed, you will receive a confirmation email/sms with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 30 days of receipt for a full refund. The item must be in its original condition and packaging. Please contact our customer service for assistance with returns.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through the "Contact Us" section on our website. We are available to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
